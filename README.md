# May3verse

A birthday celebration crafted with love for the most special person in the universe.

## What is this?

This is a heartfelt birthday wish wrapped in starlight, floating hearts, and a melody that plays every time you open it. Built to remind someone extraordinary just how deeply they are loved.

## Features

- Starry night sky with twinkling stars
- Floating hearts that drift like whispered promises
- Glittering cursor that trails magic wherever you go
- Photo slideshow with gentle transitions and sparkles
- A live age counter celebrating every second of their beautiful life
- A love letter written from the heart
- Background music that sets the mood
